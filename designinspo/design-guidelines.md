ENE Group Brand Guidelines 2026

Visual Identity & Standards for Eng Kah Enterprise (ENE Group)

01. Introduction

The ENE Vision

ENE Group (Eng Kah Enterprise) stands as a pillar of innovation in the contract manufacturing industry. Specialized in skincare, personal care, and home care, our brand represents a bridge between scientific research and consumer well-being. We don't just manufacture products; we engineer solutions that empower brands to lead their respective markets.

Purpose of These Guidelines

This document serves as the primary reference for maintaining a consistent and professional visual identity. Consistency builds trust, and as a leader in OEM/ODM manufacturing, our visual presence must reflect our core values: Reliability, Quality, and Innovation. Every touchpoint—from a digital interface to a physical shipping crate—must resonate with these standards.

02. Construction & Placement

The ENE Mark

The ENE logo is a minimalist typographic mark. The interconnected nature of the letters "ENE" symbolizes the integration of our supply chain, from Research & Development to final production.

Clarity: The logo must always have a "clear zone" equal to the height of the letter 'E' to prevent visual clutter and ensure maximum impact.

Structure: Precise, bold lines suggest industrial strength and the precision of our laboratory processes.

Reliability: The heavy weight of the font indicates a stable, long-standing enterprise that partners can depend on.

Preferred Placement

Horizontal (Primary): The standard "ENE" mark with "Eng Kah Enterprise" sub-text to the right. This is the preferred version for letterheads, website headers, and corporate signage.

Stacked (Secondary): Used when horizontal space is limited, such as on vertical banners or mobile app icons. The "ENE" mark sits centered above the corporate name.

Icon Only: The standalone "ENE" mark may be used for social media profile pictures or as a favicon, provided the brand context is already established.

03. Use & Misuse

To maintain the integrity of the ENE brand, the logo must never be altered, recolored, or distorted.

Correct Usage

Backgrounds: Place the logo on ENE Primary Blue or clean White backgrounds.

Legibility: Ensure the sub-text (Eng Kah Enterprise) is clearly legible at all scales.

Proportions: Maintain the original aspect ratio at all times.

Misuse (Forbidden)

No Distortion: Do not stretch, compress, or skew the logo.

No Color Swapping: Do not use colors outside the approved palette. Never use neon, gradients within the logo mark itself, or unauthorized primary colors.

No Shadows: Avoid drop shadows, glows, or bevel effects. The logo should remain flat and modern.

No Rotation: The logo must always sit on a 0° horizontal axis.

05. Typography

Typography at ENE Group is designed for high legibility across both digital screens and high-density product labels.

Headings: Montserrat

Style: Bold or Semi-Bold.

Application: All titles, section headers, and hero marketing copy.

Character: Geometric and authoritative, providing a modern tech-forward feel.

Body Text: Inter

Style: Regular or Medium.

Application: Reports, website descriptions, email body, and correspondence.

Character: Extremely high legibility at small sizes (e.g., ingredient lists), neutral, and professional.

06. Imagery & Tone

Visual Style

Cleanliness: Imagery should feature bright, well-lit laboratory environments with a "high-key" aesthetic.

The Human Element: Highlight our scientists, researchers, and factory floor specialists. Photos should be candid and professional, avoiding overly "staged" stock photography.

Product Clarity: Finished products should be shot with soft lighting against neutral backgrounds to emphasize packaging quality and formulation clarity.

Tone of Voice

Our communication style is Knowledgeable, Precise, and Collaborative.

Knowledgeable: We speak with the authority of decades of manufacturing experience.

Precise: We use data and scientific accuracy to describe our processes.

Collaborative: We position ourselves as an extension of our client's team, emphasizing a "Total Solution" partnership.
