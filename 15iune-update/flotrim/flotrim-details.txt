## MAINTENANCE AND RETROFITTING

We are offering the best maintenance/monitoring solution for your existing choke valve:-

1- Site Survey – for the needs of choke valve maintenance, how do we know that the valves needs attention:-
Flow Rate Through Choke Increases
      - Same position and pressures gives
        higher flow
Choke Position Decreases
      - DCS drives choke more closed to meet 
        same process requirements
Pressure Change with no Flow Change
      - ΔP decreases with no decreases in flow

2- Choke Valve Refurbishment/Retrofitting
For existing valves (manual/actuated) – to inspect on body and internal parts. If found in good condition, replace internal parts, hydro test, new paint, new warranty.
For existing manual choke valve – to upgrade to actuated choke without sacrificing the existing valve for the ease of monitoring the performance of choke valve.
Why do we propose to change internal parts and the setup of the choke valve from manual to actuation?
      - To have a spare valve
      - Easy data acquisition from the wellhead
      - Monitoring choke valve performance for
        maintenance planning
      - Easy operation
